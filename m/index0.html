CSDP 2022

4th International Workshop on Cyber Security and Data Privacy
Aug. 15 - Aug. 16 2022
Virtual, China

With the rapid development of modern information technology, it is more and more convenient for people to obtain and publish information. However, at the same time, personal information is collected, stored and distributed on a large scale, which leads to the increasingly severe situation of cyber security and privacy protection. To tackle the emerging challenges in security and privacy, we bring together researchers and practitioners, who come from different organizations and backgrounds, to share their accomplishments and experiences.

The International Workshop on Cybersecurity and Data Privacy (CSDP) aims to provide a forum to discuss the problems, the challenges, the solutions, the insights, and the vision of security and privacy in the cyberspace.
